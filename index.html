Frequently Asked Questions

1) How can I avail any of the given services?
You need to select your favourite gamer followed by the desired service and fill the required details.
Your service request will be sent to the gamer and if it is accepted, you can avail the service.
2) How much do I need to pay for the service?
The prices have been set by the gamer himself/herself , you can see the price by selecting your favourite gamer.
3) How long does it take for my service request to get completed?
You will get a notification about your service via email. This will be done within 7 days from the date of your request.
4) Will my service request surely be fulfilled?
The gamer has the authority to accept/reject the request. You will be notified via email either way.
5) Will my money get refunded if my request is rejected?
Yes, you will get a refund within 5-10 working days through the medium you initially paid from. You will be notified via email about the refund.
