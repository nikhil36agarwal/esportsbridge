ESPORTS FITNESS

Esports is one of those fields in which fitness is looked down upon. But especially in the current time, when Esports is growing at a rapid rate, sufficient fitness for gamers is very much important. A balanced diet and sufficient exercise is important for health, but many people neglect the fact that fitness plays a major factor in Esports athleteâ€™s life.

Generally, an Esports athletes daily routine includes sitting and grinding for 10-12 hours without enough break. This can pose various health risks. Overstraining the tendons, ligaments and muscles in the regions of hands, elbows, shoulders and back.


Image credit: instagram/scout_pubg

Some of the usual gamer complaints are-

  • Carpal tunnel syndrome: Pain and numbness in the area of the thumb, index and middle finger due to a constriction of the middle arm nerve
  • Cubital tunnel syndrome: Pain and numbness in the area of the little and ring finger due to compression of the ulnar nerve
  • Sciatica syndrome: Damage to the nerve fibres of the main nerve of the leg (ichias nerve)
  • Mouse elbow or shoulder: Overstraining the tendons in the arms and shoulders
  • Gamers thumb: Pain in the thumb due to overstraining of the tendons (especially when using controllers)
  • Medial epicondylitis: Pain on the inside of the elbow and forearm due to inflammation of the tendons that bend the palm of the hand towards the wrist.

Therefore, embedding fitness as a daily routine is a must for Esports athletes to avoid the above conditions. Fitness helps an Esports athlete in many ways, namely-

  • It leads to career longetivity.
  • Helps in preventing common injuries.
  • Enables quick response during an intense fight.


Image credit: instagram/fnatic_pubgm


FITNESS

The fitness routine may include a variety of exercises-

  • Cardiovascular Exercises- As an Esports athlete has to sit for 10-12 hours daily, thus there are large chances of them getting fatigued out. Therefore, cardio should be added to the daily routine to improve the endurance level of the body. Cardio can include jogging/running, rope jumping. HIIT(High intensity interval training), etc.
  • Back/Shoulder Exercises- Sitting for long hours with slouched back may result in shoulder/back pain. Therefore, Back exercises can increase back strength and thus enable the athlete to put in long grind hours. Back exercise can include Pull-ups/Chin-ups.
  • Core Exercises- Core strength is necessary for everthing. Thus, having a strong core can result in good performance. Core exercises include Plank, Sit-ups, Crunches, etc.
  • Exercises for eyes- Eyes are the most strained part of an Esports athlete’s body because of excessive screen time. Eye exercises should be incorporated in the daily fitness routine.

Fitness is not only restricted to Physical fitness, but also Mental fitness. Mental fitness also plays an important role in improving the overall performance of an Esports athlete. Generally, there is so much of adrenaline rush during these intense games that it becomes uncontrollable for the athletes. Mental fitness comes into play during these situations. Mental fitness involve various brain exercise/training for quick responses, proper sleep, listening to soothing music.

 ‘Peripheral Chart Board’ is a circular arrangement of characters that get bigger from the inside out. For the exercise the eyes are focused on the point in the middle of the drawing circles. Afterwards, the person tries to read the characters clockwise from the inside to the outside as fast as possible without taking the center out of focus.
